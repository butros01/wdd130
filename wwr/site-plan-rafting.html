Overview

Purpose

The website advertises the types of activities that are available throughout the year, sells the equipment required for rafting activities, provides necessary logistics, and advocates for river conservation. Rafting is intended to be enjoyable for everyone. We will offer personal and group trips as part of our standard package. Self-tailored trips will also be available based on your needs. Our mission is to provide you with the best adventure experience possible. All are welcome.!

Audience

Target audience:
1. Adventurous teenagers, youth and adults at their 50's
2. lower middle class for group trips
3. Upper middle class for personal trips
4. upper class income for self-tailored trips.
5. People with families
People in their youth are interested and like to experience new things, those in different socioeconomic groups have diverse spending habits, and people who are close to family want to go on an enjoyable vacation together. Everyone who is interested in rafting needs equipment or materials connected to activities on the river. To make it easier for individuals to move around, logistics are also necessary. For your amusement, we are here.
